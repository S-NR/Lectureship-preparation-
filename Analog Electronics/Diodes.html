PN Junction Diode

1. PN Junction Formation

When P-type and N-type materials are joined, diffusion of carriers creates a depletion region and barrier potential.

Material Barrier Voltage
Silicon ~0.7 V
Germanium ~0.3 V

2. Forward Bias

P connected to positive terminal and N connected to negative terminal. Depletion region width decreases.

3. Reverse Bias

P connected to negative terminal and N connected to positive terminal. Depletion region widens.

4. Diode Current Equation

I = Is ( e^(V / ηVt) - 1 )

  • Is = Reverse saturation current
  • Vt ≈ 26mV at 300K
  • η = Ideality factor

6. Breakdown Mechanisms

  • Zener Breakdown: Heavily doped, low breakdown voltage.
  • Avalanche Breakdown: Lightly doped, high breakdown voltage.
